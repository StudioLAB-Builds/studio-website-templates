Before you call us

What parents always ask.

Which pathway is right for my dancer?
If your dancer is three to five, start in Tiny Royals. If they're six and up and want one to four classes a week with a year-end recital, that's the recreational path. If they're already loving dance and want to compete and travel, Royal Court auditions are in May. Most families start recreational and move into Royal Court when the dancer asks.
How does the trial class work and is it really free?
Yes. The first class in any style is free. Pick a class on the schedule that matches your dancer's age and style interest. Arrive ten minutes early so we can introduce your dancer to the teacher.
What should my child wear to the first class?
Ballet and Tiny Royals: leotard and tights, hair pulled back. Jazz, lyrical, contemporary, tap, hip hop, and acro: fitted athletic wear, hair tied back. Bare feet are fine for the first class in any style.
What does tuition cost and what does it include?
Tiny Royals is $68 per month. Recreational classes start at $78 per month per class with discounts for multiple classes. Royal Court tuition starts at $295 per month and includes choreography, music edits, and practice apparel. Competition entry fees are separate and announced for each event.
How much are recital costumes and when are they due?
Costumes typically run $75 to $95 per class and are billed in November so deliveries arrive by April. Recital tickets are $25 per seat, reserved seating, on sale in March.
Do you offer sibling or multi-class discounts?
Yes. Ten percent off the second sibling, fifteen percent off the third. A dancer in three or more classes a week receives a five percent discount on their total tuition.
What happens if my child misses a class?
Tuition is not pro-rated for missed weeks, but we offer makeup classes in any equivalent age and style for the rest of the term. Schedule the makeup through the family portal or by phone.
How do I withdraw if it isn't working out?
Give us thirty days written notice in the portal or by email. The thirty days covers your next month's tuition, then auto-pay stops. No exit fees.
