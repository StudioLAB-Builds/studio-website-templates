Before you call us

Eight things parents always ask.

How does the trial class work and is it really free?
Yes, the first class in any style is free. Pick a class from our schedule that matches your dancer's age and style interest. Arrive ten minutes early so we can introduce your dancer to the teacher. There's no enrollment pressure after.
What does my child wear to the first class?
Ballet and Tiny Royals: a leotard and tights, hair pulled back. Jazz, lyrical, tap, hip hop, and acro: fitted athletic wear and hair tied back. Bare feet are fine for any first class. Once enrolled you'll get a style-specific uniform list.
What does monthly tuition cost and what does it include?
One class per week starts at $78 per month. Tuition covers all weekly classes, costume fittings, family portal access, and faculty messaging. Recital costumes and tickets are billed separately and announced months in advance.
How much are recital costumes and when are they due?
Costumes typically run $75 to $95 per class and are billed in November so deliveries arrive in April. Recital tickets are $25 per seat, reserved seating, on sale in March.
Do you offer sibling or multi-class discounts?
Yes. Ten percent off the second sibling, fifteen percent off the third. Five percent off your total tuition for a dancer enrolled in three or more classes a week.
What happens when my child has to miss a class?
Tuition is not pro-rated, but we offer makeup classes in any equivalent age and style for the rest of the term. Let the front desk know in advance and we'll place your dancer.
How do I audition for the competition team?
The Royal Court holds auditions every May. Dancers must be currently enrolled in at least two classes per week, including one ballet or jazz technique class. Auditions are by age group with a placement panel.
How do I withdraw if it isn't the right fit?
Give us thirty days written notice in the portal or by email. The thirty days covers your next month's tuition, then auto-pay stops. No exit fees, no awkward conversations.
