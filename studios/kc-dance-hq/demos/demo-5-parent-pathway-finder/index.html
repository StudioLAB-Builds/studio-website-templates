Questions before a trial

Answer the worries before they become barriers.

How do we know which class to try?

Start with your dancer's age and what they enjoy. The KCDanceHQ team can help match a class before you attend.

Does my child need previous dance experience?

No. Classes welcome different confidence levels, and a trial is designed to help families see what feels right.

What should we wear to the first class?

Comfortable fitted activewear is fine for a trial, with hair tied back. The team can advise on shoes and uniform once enrolled.

Where do we find the timetable?

The full 2026 timetable lives on the dedicated timetable page so it can stay current and easy to scan.
