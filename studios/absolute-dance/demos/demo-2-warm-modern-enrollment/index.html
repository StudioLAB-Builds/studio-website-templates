Class paths

Scan the options, then choose the path that fits your dancer.

Families can start with age, style, or interest. Absolute Dance offers beginner-friendly classes and deeper training for dancers ready for more.

Ages 2+

A gentle first step into movement, music, classroom rhythm, and confidence.

Ages 3-4

Playful classes that build coordination, listening, and early dance joy.

Ages 5-9

Foundational classes with more skill, structure, friendship, and performance confidence.

Ages 10+

Classes for older dancers who want technique, expression, discipline, and community.

Ballet

Technique, posture, discipline, and artistry for strong dance foundations.

Tap

Rhythm, timing, musicality, and lively footwork in an upbeat class setting.

Jazz

Energy, style, turns, leaps, and performance quality for growing dancers.

Hip-hop

Musicality, confidence, groove, and stage presence in a high-energy style.

Lyrical

Emotion, storytelling, extension, and expression for dancers ready to connect movement with music.

Tumble

Strength, coordination, flexibility, and tumbling skills in a supportive setting.

Teams

Performance and competitive pathways for dancers who want more training, teamwork, and stage experience.

Parent questions

Quick answers before you register.

If you are comparing studios or returning for a new season, these are the practical details families usually need first.

What age can my child start?
Classes are available for ages 2 and up, with age-appropriate options for young dancers, elementary students, older dancers, and teens.
How do we choose the right class?
Start with your child's age, then choose the style that feels most natural. Ballet, tap, jazz, hip-hop, lyrical, tumble, and teams give families clear entry points for different interests and goals.
Can returning families use the parent portal?
Yes. Returning families can use the Parent Portal to manage account details, classes, and payments.
Where is the studio located?
Absolute Dance is located at 5109 Sunmore Circle, Midland TX 79707. The map above links directly to the studio location.
What is the easiest next step?
Register online if you know your class direction. If you are still deciding, call 432-699-2190 or email info@absolutedance.org and the studio can help you choose.
