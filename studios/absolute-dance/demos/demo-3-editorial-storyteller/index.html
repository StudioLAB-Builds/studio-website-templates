Before you register

Answers for new families.

Still wondering? Call the studio. A real person answers, every weekday.

(432) 699-2190
What ages do you accept?
We welcome dancers ages two and up. Pre-Dance is built for our smallest students, with age-levelled classes all the way through high school.
Can my dancer try a class first?
Yes. We offer a no-pressure trial visit so your dancer can meet the teacher and the room before you commit.
What does my dancer need to wear?
Each class has a simple dress code so dancers can move safely. We send the full list the moment you register.
What does tuition look like?
Monthly tuition is based on hours per week. Multi-class and family discounts apply automatically. Full pricing lives in the parent portal.
Are recital costumes extra?
Yes. Costume fees are billed in the fall so families have time to plan. We keep choices age-appropriate and reusable when possible.
What happens if we miss a class?
Life happens. Missed classes can be made up in any equivalent class within thirty days.
