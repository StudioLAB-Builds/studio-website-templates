Who we are

An award-winning dance home, built around your dancer.

Absolute Dance has spent years helping young dancers in Midland grow into confident performers and confident young people. Families come for the teaching, and they stay because their kids genuinely look forward to class each week.

The studio runs on the belief that dedication, integrity, and a long-standing commitment to every student inspires dancers to be their best, inside and outside the studio. That is what the founder writes on the wall, and it is what every teacher carries into the room.

Parent questions

Practical answers, before you commit.

The questions families ask most often when comparing studios or starting a new season. Anything else, the front desk is happy to help.

Email a question
What age can my dancer start?
Classes are available for ages 2 and up, with age-appropriate options for tiny movers, elementary dancers, older students, and teens. The Pre-Dance and Tiny Movers programs are designed for the youngest students.
How do we choose the right class?
Start with your dancer's age, then pick the style that feels most natural for them. Ballet, tap, jazz, hip-hop, lyrical, and tumble are all available. If you're not sure, the studio team is glad to help you choose during a quick call or visit.
How do registration and payments work?
Registration is online through the family portal. You can manage your account, classes, and payments there. Returning families keep their existing portal login.
Can parents watch class?
Yes. Each studio has an observation area or window so families can watch without disrupting the lesson. There's also a homework room and lounge for siblings while class is running.
Do you offer competitive teams?
Yes. Competitive teams train year round and perform at regional competitions. Placement is by audition and is open to dancers ages 6 and up who are ready for the extra commitment.
Where is the studio?
Absolute Dance is at 5109 Sunmore Circle, Midland TX 79707. Easy parking, direct entry, and a five minute drive from most of west Midland.
